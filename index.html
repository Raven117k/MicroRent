1. Introduction

MicroRent Plus is a Flutter-based peer-to-peer micro-rental platform that enables users to borrow or lend small everyday items nearby for short durations (minutes or hours). The app supports real-time, proximity-based exchanges using NFC or Bluetooth, ensuring fast and secure handovers without shipping. This enhanced version introduces gamified rewards, dynamic platform fees, and trust-based incentives to create a self-sustaining rental economy.

2. Purpose and Scope

MicroRent Plus aims to build a hyperlocal sharing network for physical items such as:

Chargers & Power Banks

Mobile device charging solutions for emergency power needs

Umbrellas & Rain Gear

Weather protection items for unexpected rain conditions

Tools & Equipment

Basic tools for quick repairs and DIY projects

Accessories & Gadgets

Electronics and personal accessories for temporary use

The system supports secure transactions, escrow-based payments, item verification via NFC, and a reward system to encourage user engagement. The platform serves both individual users and business partners, with support for membership tiers and multi-currency transactions.
